Crunchyroll Sync - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2025

Information We Collect

Crunchyroll Sync collects and temporarily stores the following information:

  • Display Name: When you join a room, you can enter a display name that is shared with other users in the room.
  • Watch Status: When you play, pause, or seek the video, this information is shared with other users in your room.
  • Chat Messages and Reactions: Any messages or reactions you send in a room are shared with other users in that room.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing the synchronization service. Specifically:

  • Your display name is used to identify you to other users in your room.
  • Watch status information is used to synchronize video playback between users.
  • Chat messages and reactions are used to facilitate communication between users.

Data Storage and Retention

All data is stored temporarily in server memory and is automatically deleted when:

  • You leave a room
  • The room is closed (when all users leave)
  • The server restarts

We do not permanently store any user data.

Third-Party Services

Crunchyroll Sync uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Cloud Platform for hosting the synchronization server
